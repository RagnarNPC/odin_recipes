Blood Orange Vinaigrette

What is it?

The sweet tang from blood oranges makes a beautiful vinaigrette. Toss it over your favorite greens, some fresh slices of blood orange, and a few candied nuts for an amazing salad.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ⅓ cup blood orange juice
  • ¼ cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon spicy brown mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine olive oil, blood orange juice, red wine vinegar, honey, mustard, salt, and pepper in a blender. Process until well combined and smooth.
